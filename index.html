💡 White Spaces YC is Betting On

AI Agents for Physical Industries

Factories, construction sites, and logistics depots still run on spreadsheets. AI agents operating in physical-world contexts are a huge emerging bet.

AI Infra Below the App Layer

Every AI app needs evals, RAG, memory, and observability. The "AWS for AI" is still being built — pick any missing primitive.

Voice AI in Regulated Industries

Healthcare, legal, and financial services still run on phone calls. Compliance-aware AI voice agents are barely scratched.

Energy Software for AI Demand

Data centers are energy-hungry. Grid software, energy procurement, and efficiency tools for AI infrastructure are underserved.

Permitting & Regulatory Automation

Construction permitting, FDA filings, SEC compliance, zoning — all involve mountains of documents + human reviewers. Pure LLM wins here.

🔴 Crowded / Risky Territories

Generic AI Dev Tools / LLM Wrappers

The "AI for X" horizontal layer is overcrowded. Without deep workflow integration or proprietary data, these compress fast.

Consumer Social Apps

YC has clearly pulled back — only 2.2% of W26 vs 5.4% in Su25. The distribution problem for consumer is unsolved post-AppStore.

Generic Sales AI / SDR Bots

Every company is building AI outbound. Signal-to-noise is collapsing as inboxes fill with AI emails.

EdTech (generic)

Declining from 4.2% (W25) to 1.6% (W26). Consumer education is hard to monetize; enterprise upskilling is crowded.

Basic AI Chatbots / Copilots

The era of "add ChatGPT to your product" is over. Multi-step agentic systems that take real actions are the new bar.
