📡 What This Says About the Future of Technology & Startups

🚀 The AI-Native Shift

Every batch since W2023 shows accelerating "AI-first" thinking. The dominant archetypes are no longer pure SaaS products — they're AI agents, LLM tooling, and vertical AI. Startups are being funded not for building software, but for building intelligence layers.

🔮 Where YC Is Betting Next

Agentic workflows, voice AI, and vertical AI in healthcare/legal/finance dominate recent batches. The "picks and shovels" play (AI infra) peaked early — now the value is moving up the stack into applications with deep domain expertise.

💡 Ideation Signal

Gaps exist in physical-world AI (supply chain, construction, field services) and regulated industry automation (insurance, government). The next wave may be AI solving problems that require real-world data and compliance moats.

⚡ Speed of Change

YC batch compositions are shifting faster than any prior era. Themes that were niche in W2023 (AI agents) are now dominant in W2026. Founders have ~18 months before a theme becomes crowded.
